Item Description

Restore smooth and quiet operation to your dryer with this genuine OEM Dryer Drum Glide Bearing (2-Pack). Identified as Diagram ID 317, these durable bearing slides are installed on the front panel to support the drum as it rotates, significantly reducing friction and wear between the drum and the housing. Over time, these glides can thin or become damaged due to heat and regular use, leading to squeaking, scraping noises, or inefficient drum rotation. This high-quality replacement part is designed to provide stability and quiet performance for your laundry cycles. Note: It is recommended to replace all bearing slides simultaneously to ensure even wear and optimal dryer longevity.
